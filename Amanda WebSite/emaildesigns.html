General Assembly Email Redesign

In previous versions of General Assembly emails, the templates were inconsistent, lacked direction from the brand's guidelines, and plain + simple were an OCD designer's nightmare. I was tasked to redesign these emails with the goal of creating one template that was made up of modules easy to move around. The immediate goal was to create these to be on-brand without sacrificing previous email perfomance. (Since these emails have gone out, overall performance has improved).
